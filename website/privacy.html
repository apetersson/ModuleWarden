Privacy

Build window stub. Last updated 2026-05-28.

What this page is

ModuleWarden was built at Zero-One Hack Vienna 2026. This site is a static landing page with no backend, no analytics, and no cookies. There is nothing to opt out of here.

What we collect via this site

  • Nothing on page load. No analytics SDK, no fingerprinting script, no Google Tag Manager. The static files come from GitHub Pages.
  • When you submit the early-access form your browser opens its mail client with a prefilled mailto:hello@modulewarden.com. The form fields are pasted into the mail body. We receive only what you choose to send when you click the email send button.

What the product itself collects

ModuleWarden the product is a self-hosted npm registry proxy plus a per-job audit pipeline. The audit pipeline reads:

  • npm package tarballs from the public registry (one tarball per audit)
  • package.json fields, lifecycle scripts, dependency graph metadata
  • The artifacts the per-job audit container produces (Control Evidence Memo, audit report JSON)

Self-host deployments keep all of the above inside the operator's network. No data is sent to the maintainers of ModuleWarden.

Hosted (cloud) deployments

Cloud deployments are not in scope for the hackathon window. When those become available we will publish a full DPA, retention policy, and DPO contact here.
