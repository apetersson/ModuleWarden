# 3 LEONARDO Account
Source: https://ai-at.eu/hpc-onboarding/chapter-3

---

Before you can log in to LEONARDO or any other CINECA supercomputer, you need to set up a **CINECA UserDB account** and enable **two-factor authentication (2FA)**. This ensures secure and verified access to high-performance computing (HPC) resources.

* * *

**1. Creating a CINECA UserDB Account**

Start by visiting the **UserDB portal** at https://userdb.hpc.cineca.it. Click **"Create new user"** and fill in your personal details. You will also need to upload a valid **identity document** (such as a passport or ID card) in the "Documents for HPC" section, and provide your **institutional affiliation**. Once this information is saved, your account registration is complete — but you do not yet have access to HPC systems.

* * *

**2. Linking Your Account to a Project**

Access to CINECA systems is always granted through a **project association**.

-   If you are a **Principal Investigator (PI)**, you can request and manage your own project.
-   If you are a **collaborator**, you will need the PI to add you to an existing project.

Once your account is linked to a project, this information will appear in your UserDB profile.

* * *

**3. Requesting HPC Access**

After confirming your project association, open the **"HPC Access"** tab in the UserDB portal. The portal checks that all required fields (personal data, institution, identity document, and project link) are complete. When everything is in order, the **"Submit"** button becomes active, allowing you to request HPC access.

Once your request is approved, CINECA sends two emails:

1.  One with your assigned **HPC username**, and
2.  Another with a link to configure **two-factor authentication (2FA)** and set your password.

⚠️ The 2FA activation link is typically valid for **12 hours**, so be sure to complete the process promptly.

* * *

**4. Enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**

Follow the link in the email to the 2FA setup page. You'll be prompted to:

1.  Create your **HPC password**, and
2.  Enable **One-Time Password (OTP)** generation.

To activate OTP, scan the QR code displayed on screen using an authenticator app such as **FreeOTP** or **Google Authenticator**, then enter the code shown in the app. Once successful, you'll receive a set of **recovery codes** — store them safely for future use.

CINECA requires **2FA for all HPC access** — you cannot log in using only a password.

For further information, have a look at the CINECA documentation
